About

Hi! I’m Raiyani, a Computer Science graduate with a degree in Network Computing from Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS). I’m passionate about building frontend interfaces and connected systems, especially in IoT and networking projects. During my studies, I designed systems like an IoT-based crack detection and monitoring platform, working with sensors, cloud services, and real-time data visualization. These projects taught me how to think beyond code — designing solutions that are practical, reliable, and user-friendly. I love the intersection of design and technology: crafting interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while ensuring usability, accessibility, and seamless connections to the bigger networked system. Outside of coding, I value learning, collaboration, and creating solutions people actually enjoy using. I’m always looking to grow my skills and bring ideas to life in creative, functional ways.
